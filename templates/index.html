Customer Segments Explained

Budget Conscious

Price-sensitive customers who focus on value and deals. They research thoroughly before making purchases and prefer cost-effective solutions.

High Value

Customers with high income and significant spending power. They make frequent, high-value purchases and are less price-sensitive.

Regular Customers

Consistent customers with moderate spending patterns. They maintain steady purchasing behavior and show balanced engagement.

Premium Shoppers

Quality-focused customers who prefer premium products. They value brand reputation and are willing to pay more for superior quality.

Occasional Buyers

Customers who make infrequent but meaningful purchases. They often buy during special occasions or when specific needs arise.

Loyal Customers

Highly engaged customers with frequent interactions. They show strong brand loyalty and often become brand advocates.
